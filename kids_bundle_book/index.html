About SpacECE India Foundation

"Empowering Futures Through Early Childhood Excellence"

SpacECE India Foundation is dedicated to early childhood education for children aged 0–8 years — the most critical period for cognitive, social, and emotional growth. We believe that the foundations of all learning are laid during these transformative early years. Our work aligns with India's National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 and the National ECCE Policy, and supports the national goal of ensuring every child entering Grade 1 is school-ready by 2030.

Our Approach: Learning That Grows With Your Child

SpacECE follows a longitudinal progression model — activities at each stage build upon the previous milestones, ensuring consistency and cumulative growth from home to preschool to school.

1

Enhance your child's learning journey by creating a stimulating home environment that fosters curiosity, exploration, and age-appropriate skill building for ages 1–3.

2

3

School Support

Ensure holistic academic development by reinforcing school concepts at home through structured worksheets, tracing exercises, and creative activities.
